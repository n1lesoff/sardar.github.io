About Me

Design with Purpose

I believe that every detail in interior design tells its own story. I am Sardar Gurbanzade, an architectural designer who creates spaces that not only look beautiful but also offer comfort and inspiration. Inspired by different cultures and architectural styles, I design unique and functional environments. I meet challenges with creativity and implement ideas using CAD, 3D modeling, and rendering. My goal is to contribute to contemporary interior architecture on an international level.

Education & Experience

Foundation & Growth

I studied architecture and design through various specialized courses in Azerbaijan. This allowed me to combine the development of my creative vision with gaining up-to-date technical skills. Furthermore, I completed an internship at Ra Construction, where I gained hands-on experience in functional design principles and worked on real-world projects. This foundation enables me to design spaces that are not only visually appealing but also comfortable and practical.

I offer comprehensive architectural and interior design services including space planning, 3D visualization, material selection, furniture layout, and full project coordination from concept to completion.

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A single room redesign typically takes 2–4 weeks, while a full apartment or villa project can take 1–3 months from concept to final renders.
